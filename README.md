# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 8, 2026

Kali POS ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Kali POS collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Kali POS mobile application.

By using Kali POS, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

Kali POS may collect and process the following categories of information:

### A. Account Information

When you create or use an account, we may collect:

- Your email address
- Your Firebase authentication account information
- Your email verification status

### B. Shop and Business Information

When you use the app, you may enter and store:

- Shop name
- Shop address
- Shop phone number
- Shop footer or invoice message
- Theme and currency preferences

### C. Inventory and Sales Data

Kali POS stores information you create in the app, including:

- Products and services
- Barcodes
- Prices
- Stock quantities
- Held carts
- Invoices and invoice history

### D. Device and Local App Data

The app stores data locally on your device to make the app work properly and remain usable between sessions.

### E. Camera Access

Kali POS may access your device camera only when you choose to scan product barcodes.

### F. Shared Content

If you choose to share an invoice through WhatsApp, the invoice content you choose to send will be passed to WhatsApp or your selected sharing destination.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

- Create and manage your account
- Authenticate you and keep your session secure
- Save your shop settings and preferences
- Let you manage products, services, carts, and invoices
- Scan barcodes using your camera
- Back up and restore your data when cloud backup is available
- Let you share invoices through WhatsApp when you choose to do so
- Provide customer support and troubleshoot issues

## 3. Where Your Data Is Stored

Kali POS uses both local device storage and cloud storage.

### A. Local Device Storage

The app stores business data locally on your device using on-device storage so you can continue using the app efficiently.

### B. Cloud Backup

If you sign in, Kali POS may back up certain account and business data to Google Firebase Cloud Firestore, including:

- Account email
- Email verification status
- Shop settings
- Products
- Services
- Invoices
- Held carts

This backup is used to help restore your data and keep your account data available across sessions or after reinstalling the app.

## 4. Third-Party Services

Kali POS uses third-party services to operate core functionality:

- **Google Firebase Authentication** for account sign-in and account management
- **Google Cloud Firestore** for cloud backup and restore
- **WhatsApp / external sharing apps** when you choose to share invoice content

These third parties may process information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Kali POS does not currently include third-party advertising SDKs or in-app analytics SDKs in the current version of the app.

## 5. Premium Subscriptions

Kali POS may offer optional premium subscription features through Google Play.

Payments and subscription management are handled securely by Google Play Billing. Kali POS does not collect or store users’ credit or debit card information.

## 6. Permissions We Use

Kali POS may request the following permissions:

- **Camera:** used to scan product barcodes

The app does not use camera access unless you actively open a barcode scanning feature.

## 7. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information only in the following situations:

- With service providers such as Firebase, to provide authentication and backup functionality
- With WhatsApp or another app, only when you intentionally choose to share invoice content
- If required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request
- To protect the rights, safety, and security of Kali POS, our users, or others

## 8. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the app’s functionality.

- Local data may remain on your device until you delete it, uninstall the app, or clear app storage
- Cloud backup data may remain associated with your account until it is deleted
- If you delete your account from within the app, we intend to delete your Firebase account and cloud backup data associated with that account

## 9. Account Deletion

Kali POS includes an in-app account deletion feature.

When you delete your account from inside the app:

- Your Firebase Authentication account may be deleted
- Your cloud backup data in Firestore may be deleted

Please note:

- Data that remains on your physical device may not be removed automatically in every situation unless app storage is cleared or the app is removed
- Information you previously shared through third-party apps such as WhatsApp is controlled by those third-party services after sharing

## 10. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information used by Kali POS. However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 11. Children's Privacy

Kali POS is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 12. International Data Transfers

Because Kali POS uses Firebase and third-party service providers, your information may be processed or stored on servers located outside your country.

## 13. Your Choices

You may choose to:

- Create or not create an account
- Edit your shop and inventory data inside the app
- Use or not use barcode scanning
- Use or not use cloud backup by signing into an account
- Delete your account from within the app

## 14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.
